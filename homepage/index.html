The Sense of Locality

Why Bayside Is Different

  • Bayside, unlike the rest of NYC, is very suburban.
  • We have a deep connection with our neighbors, always.
  • The businesses are more local, more family-oriented.
  • The smell of the grass and trees are both very prevalent.
  • The houses are more old-fashioned.
